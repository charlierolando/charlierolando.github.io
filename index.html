About

Mechatronics Engineering Final-Year Student

  • Summary

I am a Mechatronics Engineering student who has knowledge in mechanics, electronics, and programming to create innovative solutions. With my strong educational background, especially in programming, I have a deep understanding of the basic principles required to design and develop complex mechatronics systems.

During my studies, I was actively involved in research and development projects at Barelang Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Lab (BRAIL), which gave me practical experience in designing, prototyping, and programming mechatronics systems, especially for embedded systems and humanoid robots. I also had the honor to participate in student competitions (DIKTI) at the national level several times. Additionally, I have good interpersonal skills and can work in a team to complete projects efficiently. I have a strong interest in robotics and automation, and I am passionate about developing solutions that can help increase efficiency and productivity across a variety of industries. With a combination of technical knowledge, communication skills and a passion for continuous learning, I am ready to face challenges in the world of mechatronics engineering and contribute to the development of future technologies.
