This project is under the auspices of the Barelang Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Lab (BRAIL) State Polytechnic of Batam. The target of this project is to take part in the 2023 Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa (PKM) organized by the Direktorat Jenderal Pendidikan Tinggi, Riset dan Teknologi Kemdikbudristek. Our team has succeeded in obtaining funding for the Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa - Riset Eksakta (PKM-RE, Student Creativity Program - Exact Research) in 2023 funding scheme.
