Humanoid Soccer Robot. detail

Project information

  • Category: Research and Development
  • Client: Politeknik Negeri Batam
  • Project date: Aug 2023 - Present
  • Project URL: polibatam.id/BFC

This project is under the auspices of the Barelang Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Lab (BRAIL) State Polytechnic of Batam. The target of this project is to participate in the Kontes Robot Sepak Bola Indonesia - Humanoid (KRSBI Humanoid), which features humanoid robots that can playing soccer. KRSBI Humanoid is an annual student competition activity in the field of robotics design and engineering organized by the National Achievement Center, Ministry of Education, Culture, Research and Technology of the Republic of Indonesia.
