This project is under the auspices of the Barelang Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Lab (BRAIL) State Polytechnic of Batam. The target of this project is to participate in the Kontes Robot Seni Tari Indonesia (KRSTI), which features humanoid robots that can dance based on dance movements and national culture in Indonesia. KRSTI is an annual student competition activity in the field of robotics design and engineering organized by the National Achievement Center, Ministry of Education, Culture, Research and Technology of the Republic of Indonesia.

The project is still ongoing, but I am no longer actively involved in it. While I was still actively involved in this project, we had won the West Indonesia level contest and always qualified for the national level. In 2021 this contest was held online due to the Covid-19 pandemic, in 2022 I participated as a core team that competed at the national level which was held in Surabaya, Indonesia, and also in 2023 which was held in Semarang, Indonesia.
